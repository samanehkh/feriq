Clinical Operations Engine

6. Clinical Triage Dashboard — Real-Time Compliance

The Clinical Triage Dashboard is the central command center for nursing teams, moving clinics from reactive patient communication to proactive, automated compliance oversight.

By consolidating patient app logs, voice stress analytics, and dose confirmations, the dashboard highlights compliance anomalies and dropout risks before they affect cycles.

  • Adherence Anomaly Detection: AI automatically flags missed doses, incorrect dosage amounts, and timing offsets.
  • Predictive Dropout Triage: Uses CalmSeed voice stress trends to highlight patients at risk of abandoning their treatment.
  • Auto-Triage Priority Queue: Ranks patient messages by urgency, letting nurses focus on clinical risks rather than repetitive admin.
  • Seamless EMR Integration: Synchronizes compliance history, stress data, and notes directly into patient records.
