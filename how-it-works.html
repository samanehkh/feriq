Connected IVF Ecosystem

How Ovify Works

Ovify integrates patient care coordination by connecting patients, support networks, and clinics in a single ecosystem, reducing compliance errors and nurse workload.

The System Components

The Three Apps in Harmony

Replacing complexity and chaos with a structured, calm, and AI-supported system.

1. Patient App

White-Labeled Patient Portal

Translates complex clinic prescriptions into secure, easy-to-follow daily checklists to boost adherence.

  • Step-by-step injection & medication guides
  • Fail-safe reminders for critical dosing windows
  • CalmSeed AI voice stress screening
  • Self-guided nervous-system regulation
  • Instant clinic timeline synchronization

2. Support App

Connected Support Portal

Engages partners in daily coordination to divide the cycle burden and reduce patient anxiety.

  • Daily share-the-load coordination checklists
  • Dose prep reminders for support partners
  • Clear clinical milestone & appointment guidance
  • Automated alerts when patient stress levels spike
  • Empathy prompts for relationship stabilization

3. Clinic App

Efficiency, Clarity, & Real-Time Insights

Empowers clinical teams to build protocols in seconds, push updates instantly, and track adherence in real time.

  • EMR-friendly, 60-second protocol builder
  • Instant 1-click timeline update deployments
  • Escalating alerts for missed patient doses
  • Real-time patient stress and compliance tracking
  • Predictive AI dashboard for dropout risk management

Patient

Support

The Comparison

Bridging the Coordination Gap

Transition your clinic from fragmented patient messaging to automated adherence intelligence.

The Fragmented Experience

  • Dosing Guesswork Rushed schedules cause patient anxiety, dosing errors, and compromised cycles.

  • Partner Disconnection Support networks lack schedule details, leaving them helpless and unable to assist.
  • Nurse Overload Nursing teams are buried under repetitive messages with zero adherence visibility.

  • AI Patient App Converts complex protocols into clear daily tasks with CalmSeed AI stress support.
  • Connected Support App Shares milestone alerts and tasks so partners can coordinate and assist.
  • Clinic Dashboard Predicts dropout risk and flags adherence anomalies in real time.
